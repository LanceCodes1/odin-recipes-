Description

A quick and easy seafood dish made with shrimp, garlic, butter, and lemon. Ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Season shrimp with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and add garlic.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Squeeze lemon over shrimp.
  5. Serve immediately.
