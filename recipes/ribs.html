Description

These oven baked bbq ribs are tender, flavorful, and easy to make without a grill.

Ingredients

  • Rack of ribs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Season ribs with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Wrap ribs in foil.
  3. Bake at 300Â°F for 3 hours.
  4. Brush with BBQ sauce.
  5. Broil until sauce caramelizes.
