Description

A simple classic lasagna made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. Perfect for an easy family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 40 minutes.
  5. Let rest before serving.
